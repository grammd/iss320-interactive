# Author - Ferdinand Tönnies

## Bio
Ferdinand Tönnies (1855–1936) was a German sociologist known for the distinction between Gemeinschaft and Gesellschaft.

## Key Ideas
- [[Concept - Gemeinschaft]] (community) vs. [[Concept - Gesellschaft]] (society) as ideal types of social organization.
- Rural/traditional vs. urban/contractual relations.
