# Reading - toennies.pdf

Excerpts on [[Concept - Gemeinschaft]] and [[Concept - Gesellschaft]], [[Concept - Binary Opposition]]. Tönnies used these categories to classify social relationships: Gesellschaft is associated with modern society and rational self-interest, which weakens the traditional bonds of family and local community that typify Gemeinschaft; Max Weber wrote in direct response to Tönnies.
