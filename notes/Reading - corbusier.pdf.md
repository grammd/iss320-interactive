# Reading - corbusier.pdf

Summary/excerpts from the Corbusier reading. Covers [[Concept - Man's Way]], [[Concept - Pack-Donkey's Way]], and rational urban planning. Le Corbusier was a pioneer of modern architecture and a founding member of CIAM; his urban planning ideas emphasised better living conditions in crowded cities through rational design, though they have also been criticised for indifference to pre-existing cultural sites.
