# Author - Lewis Mumford

## Bio
Lewis Mumford (1895â€“1990) was an American historian, sociologist, and critic of architecture/urbanism. Championed organic, human-centered planning influenced by Patrick Geddes and Ebenezer Howard.

## Key Ideas
- City as a "Theater of Social Action" and facilitator of "Social Drama."
- Emphasizes social relationships over economic functions; cities should enhance human personality and spiritual values.
- Critiques overemphasis on physical design without social context.
